Sanremo 2021, Amadeus: "It will be a Festival as normal as possible". On the guests: "I'm thinking about another reunion"

Reference site about Lorem Ipsum, giving information on its origins, as well as a random Lipsum generator.

By Fabio Fabbretti

Amadeus

The mission, at least in words, is simple: to go on air from 2 to 6 March 2021 and guarantee the show that everyone expects, respecting the health rules necessary to deal with the pandemic. The 71st Sanremo Festival will have to represent the restart of a sector, the music and entertainment one, tormented by Covid and for this reason Amadeus does not "give in" to sacrifices: the festival has to be done at its best, as every year.

The Festival must be from 2 to 6 March (…) Nobody must be put at risk, but we must work to make a Festival as normal as possible. Sanremo you cannot do it without an audience (...) the music sector has gone to the ground, there are people who have been unemployed for months, we have to relaunch music, entertainment and theater, Sanremo must also help theaters, I would like to rekindle theaters in all of Italy right from Sanremo.

the conductor told RTL.102.5. Hence the question of the ship that will host the audience of the Ariston Theater for the five evenings:

The idea is to create a bubble for at least the 500 people in the audience, which are always the same, so that they can attend the evenings and to ensure that this happens they must be protected and have no contact with anyone . They will spend a week of vacation on the ship with daily tampons and in the evening they will be taken to the theater with minibuses of up to 20 people, it is not easy, but it can be done. It is an idea that is becoming concrete, in the sense that we are seriously working on it and I have known that they are curious from all over the world , because it has never happened.

It does not exclude that among the public there may also be vaccinated health workers , so as to convey an important message ( “the vaccine is the weapon we have to fight the virus, we have to make many more vaccines, we have to travel” , he says). On the entertainment front, Amadeus speaks for the first time about the participation of Achille Lauro and Zlatan Ibrahimovic alongside him :

Achille Lauro will make five artistic paintings that will make a journey through music and over time, it will be a moment of great show, he is a complete artist (...) Zlatan is an incredible character with a great charisma, he is transversal, he is one of those characters who do not belong to a team, but to world football and is also very nice. I will probably entrust it to Fiorello, who has already told me 'I have some ideas about Ibrahimovic ... but I won't tell you'.
