Q: DM: what it is, content and nature?
What is DM?

Davide Maggio's Blog is a television critic's blog that is concerned, among other things, with providing its readers with curiosity, previews and indiscretions on everything that goes around television.

An alternative and unfiltered way of providing information. An uncensored “pen” that tries to reveal and tell what traditional newspapers don't write.

Copyright

The photo-audio-video material present on DM is found on the internet and, therefore, evaluated in the public domain. If the authors or any copyright holders have something against the publication, they will only have to report it to the owner of the blog (via email: mail @ davidemaggio.it) who will promptly remove it; the articles are covered by copyright and readers are allowed to freely “copy and paste them” provided the source is indicated.

Q: Post
TAGs

TAGs are a very useful tool. They are like labels, keywords that you find at the end of each post and identify the content in a more specific way than the categories. By clicking on the tag you are interested in, you will read all the posts on davidemaggio.it linked to the selected topic.

Feed RSS

RSS feeds are a useful tool to read davidemaggio.it posts faster

There are two ways to read davidemaggio.it via RSS feed:

1. Download a special program (FEED Reader) that reads the RSS feeds (blog posts, in essence) and enter the appropriate address: http://feeds.feedburner.com/DavideMaggio

2. In some browsers (eg IE 7.x or Firefox) the FEED reader is included. So just save the address you just provided in your favorites and that's it.

Share

At the end of each post there is the "Share" function thanks to which you can report the post via email to a friend and / or aggregate it thanks to the most famous social bookmark services in the world.
